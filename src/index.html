The Textile Makerspace does not have an ongoing source of funding for student staffing. In order to ensure we can have at least some open hours next academic year, the Textile Makerspace will be closed during summer 2026.

Wish it were open this summer? Us too. We've got a brief form you can fill out to share your thoughts and help us make the case for funding for the space.

In the meantime, our un-staffed space for yarn-based crafts (YarnLab) will be open during regular business hours on the 4th floor of Wallenberg, in the red quadrant of CESTA.
