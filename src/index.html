The Textile Makerspace located in building 260 (Pigott Hall) in room 238 is open somewhat regular hours, for anyone to come use our sewing machines, serger, embroidery machine, knitting machines, looms, and other making supplies. If the building is locked, you can call 650-736-3626 for someone to let you in.
