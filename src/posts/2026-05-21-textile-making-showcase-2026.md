On June 5 from 1-4 PM, we will be holding the third annual Textile Dataviz Showcase, with contributions from students in *DLCL 203: Data Visualization with Textiles*, as well as other Stanford makers. The showcase will be held in Hohbach Hall 126 (first floor of Green Library).

If you'd like to share some of your work in the exhibit, please [fill out this form](https://forms.gle/WKGbpAbam99YGqMw8) by Tuesday, June 2st and we'll follow up with details. Please contact Quinn Dombrowski (qad@stanford.edu) with any questions.
