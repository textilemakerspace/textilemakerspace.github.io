    Textile Makerspace

    Pigott Hall, 260-238

    The Textile Makerspace located in building 260 (Pigott Hall) in room 238 is open somewhat regular hours, for anyone to come use our sewing machines, serger, embroidery machine, and other sewing supplies. If the building is locked, you can call 650-736-3626 for someone to let you in.

    For yarn-based crafts, YarnLab is on the 4th floor of Wallenberg, in the red quadrant of CESTA. The red cabinets by the windows have yarn, needle felting supplies, small looms, tapestry needles, and other materials for yarn crafts. Circular knitting machines are available on the tables, along with crochet hooks and knitting needles that you can take home. YarnLab is open during CESTA hours (regular business hours) and will have staffed hours soon.
